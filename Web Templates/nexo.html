What is the Nexo Exchange?

The Nexo Exchange is Nexo’s swap functionality, which allows for immediate exchanges between 100+ crypto and fiat pairs directly within the Nexo platform. The feature is a one-stop-shop for the NEXO Token and other crypto and fiat currencies, enabling users to buy, sell, store, borrow against, and earn interest on their assets.

How can Nexo guarantee the best market prices currently available?

Thanks to our in-house Smart Routing system, the Nexo Exchange simultaneously connects to 5+ exchanges to identify the best prices and splits orders depending on the price per volume. The system optimizes swapping between fiat and crypto currencies within a fraction of a second and creates a deal better than any manual execution.

What currencies does the Nexo Exchange support?

The Nexo Exchange currently supports swaps between more than 100 digital and fiat currency pairs, including these NEXO Token pairs:

  • NEXO / BTC
  • NEXO / ETH
  • NEXO / USDT
  • NEXO / USD
  • NEXO / EUR
  • NEXO / GBP

See all available pairs on our help center.

Note: Trading NEXO Tokens on the Nexo Exchange requires Advanced Verification and is currently not available to US citizens.

How can I buy and sell NEXO Tokens?

The Nexo Exchange eliminates the need for third-party services for buying and selling NEXO Tokens. To buy or sell NEXO Tokens you need to:

  1. Open the Nexo platform or the Nexo Wallet App
  2. Click on the “Exchange” tab
  3. Select the currencies you would like to swap
  4. Click “Exchange”
